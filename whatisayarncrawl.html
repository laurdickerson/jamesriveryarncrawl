What is a Yarn Crawl & other FAQs

What is a Yarn Crawl?

We’re glad you asked! A Yarn Crawl is a trip to see several local yarn shops. In our case, we’ll be visiting local yarn shops all along the James River, tracting the path of this mighty river through Virginia.

Are there special prizes for visiting each shop?

You’d better believe it! We’ll be handing out passports that you can stamp at each shop. If you fill out every slot on your passport, you get a special surprise for making it to the end!

How can I participate?

Come visit each shop along the way. We’ll be giving away prizes, hanging out and knitting, and celebrating our fiber community across Virginia!
